Stay Strong and Independent

Take a few minutes to check in with your physical well-being. These simple assessments can help you understand if there are areas where you might benefit from extra support to maintain your independence.

In order for us to understand your mobility and physical well-being please complete at least two of the assessments below. How your responses are interpreted will depend upon your age and gender so please begin by entering these.


PRISMA 7 Questionnaire

Gait Speed Test

The Gait Speed Test is a simple assessment of functional mobility which can be administered by a Healthcare Professional. Please enter your latest Gait Speed Test time in the area below.


Timed-Up-and-Go (TUG)

The Timed Up and Go (TUG) test is a simple and effective way for clinicians to evaluate your mobility and balance. It helps clinicians to understand your risk of falling and your overall physical functioning.
